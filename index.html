Performance Bounds

Compute-bound: Matrix Multiplication

\[\text{C}_{ij} = \sum_{k} A_{ik} B_{kj}\]\[\text{AI} = \frac{2n^3}{2n^2 \times \texttt{sizeof}(A)} = \frac{n}{8}\]

Memory-bound: Field update

\[\phi_{ijk} = \phi_{ijk} + \Delta t \mathcal{F}_{ijk}\]\[\text{AI} = \frac{2n}{2n \times \texttt{sizeof} (\phi)} = \frac{1}{8}\]
